How you can prevent more plastic from ending up in the sea?

Don’t contribute to fast fashion

A lot of fast fashion ends up in the ocean.

Leave Nothing Behind

Only leave your footprints in the sand when you go to the beach, not your trash.

Use reuseable cloth bags instead of plastic bags

If you try to bring a reuseable cloth bag to the grocery store and use it, you might eliminate one plastic bag.

RECYCLE

Recycle the plastic that can be recycled so it can be reused for somthing else.

Buy Bio-Degratable plastic items if possible

Ususally plastic products in the 21st century are made of thinner more desoulable plastic; Some products like chip, screens, and cheese need a more flexible type of plastic.

Buy groceries in bulk when you can

When you buy items in bulk you can get the amount of food that you need, and you can buy your items in glass jars. It’s also cheaper.
